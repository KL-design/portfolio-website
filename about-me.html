Curious to know a bit more about me?

I’m a born and raised Canadian hailing from the Muskoka area in Ontario.

I’m a dog mom and big outdoors enthusiast. The things I’m known for among family and friends are being the artsy, creative one that hunts and knows a weird amount about dogs.

Career-wise, before I decided to pursue my passion for creativity and design, I took a detour.

I got my Bachelor of Science with honours in Animal Biology from the University of Guelph (that’s right, I’m an Aggie Alum!). I enjoyed that program but, while my course load was heavily focused in animal anatomy, physiology, and nutrition, I made sure to take the opportunity with my electives to study things that really captured my interest as well, French language and psychology.

After that, I did a 6 year stint with the Ontario Provincial Police where I provided admin support for one of the busiest forensic units in the province. Forensics was cool, for sure, and I thought my career was going to be with the police but, I couldn’t stop thinking about design and web design.

Once I discovered UX, UI, and graphic design in my research, it was game over. They quickly captured my focus. I finally got up the courage to become a career-changer and haven’t looked back. It was one of the best decisions I’ve ever made!
